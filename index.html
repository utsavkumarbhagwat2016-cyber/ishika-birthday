Then we met again in the college auditorium. My leg accidentally touched yours again! 🤦‍♂️ This time I just got super shy 😳. I quickly said sorry without even looking at you and rushed away. When I look back, those small, clumsy leg bumps and whispered apologies were the funny, sweet start of everything. It makes me smile to know that our first real steps toward each other were just our feet meeting under a table. 💖.
Our first official meet-up, after all those calls, started out so stiff, but watching Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani soon melted the tension. I loved watching you try to hold back your laughter when I made my lame jokes. Trying to hold your hand felt so awkward at first, but making you laugh again and When I nervously joked, "You won't hit me, right?"—"Zyada kiya toh ho sakta hai"—made me pause and just look at you. It was the way the light caught your glasses, making you shine, and I realized I didn't care that I usually didn't like girls in glasses—you just looked perfect. I was so confused by the intensity of what I was feeling! Later, over our long talk at McDonald's, the surprise spark of jealousy I felt when Abhishek called proved to me that something real and deep was beginning. That powerful moment of noticing you is sealed forever in the picture I took that day, a memory I will never, ever delete. ✨.
